SDG categorisation is assigned based on documented activities, not self-reported by initiatives. An initiative is tagged under one main SDG, but can be tagged under two if both are equally covered by the initiative SDGs outside this environmental scope (e.g. SDG 4 Education) are not tracked as primary categories.

The Ocean Blindspot

SDG 14 — Life Below Water — is the least represented SDG across Southeast Asia, despite the region containing 34% of the world's coral reefs and being home to some of the world's most ocean-dependent communities.

Topic Distribution

What youth are paying attention to — and what they're ignoring. The dominance of waste management suggests a bias towards visible, tangible, downstream problems over invisible, systemic, upstream ones.

Action vs. Advocacy

The overwhelming majority of initiatives focus on direct action — cleanups, community projects, hands-on work. Systemic change through advocacy and policy engagement is a marginal activity.
